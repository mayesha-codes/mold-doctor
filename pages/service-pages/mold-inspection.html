Why Mold Inspection Matters

Mold can be a silent threat, growing unnoticed in hidden areas of your home or commercial property. Our professional mold inspection services utilize cutting-edge technology, including infrared cameras and moisture meters, to detect potential mold colonies before they become a major issue.

When Should You Get a Mold Inspection?

  • You notice a persistent musty odor indoors
  • You've had recent water damage or leaks
  • You’re experiencing unexplained allergies or respiratory issues
  • You’re buying or selling a home or property

What Our Inspection Includes

Our certified specialists perform a comprehensive evaluation of your property, including:

  • Interior & exterior visual mold inspection
  • Humidity and moisture level testing
  • Infrared thermal imaging scans
  • Detailed report with findings and recommendations
